Scrambled Eggs with Bacon


Recipe by Simon

Scrambled Eggs with Bacon is a simple dish to cook that immediatly satisfies your stomach and cheers you up when you are at your lowest points.

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Bacon
  • Butter

Directions:

  1. First, melt a bit of butter in the pan
    2. ! Remember not to overheat the pan and keep it at a medium heat!
  2. While the butter is melting, break the eggs open into a bowl
  3. Whisk until the yolk and the white part of the egg are combined and then pour them into the pan
  4. Now before the egg turns solid, add the bacon
  5. Lastly, enjoy your meal
